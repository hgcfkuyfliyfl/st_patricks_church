Who we are?

We are a dedicated community of believers committed to living out the teachings of Jesus Christ through worship, fellowship, and service. Our church is a place where you can find hope, support, and spiritual growth.

Believes

We believe in the Trinity, the Bible as God's Word, salvation through faith in Jesus Christ, God's grace, and the importance of community.

Our creed

We believe in one God, Jesus Christ as our Savior, the Holy Spirit, the universal Church, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection, and eternal life.
